    OpenAiChain Chain Creates a Shared Artificial Intelligence Platform

    Let OpenAIChain：Accelerate the development of artificial intelligence industry

    Goal overview

    OpenAiChain as a Next Generation Blockchain Platform Combines Blockchain Technology with Artificial Intelligence,
    to establish a global data incentive, academic paper source and knowledge sharing platform
    • Build a National Data Acquisition Platform
    • Build AI knowledge sharing, question and answer platform
    • Tracing the source of college papers
    • Shared computing power and storage platform
    • Model and Algorithm Trading Platform
    • Cross-domain multi-party cooperation platform
    • Artificial Intelligence Education Project

    Build a National Data Acquisition Platform

    OpenAIChain's biggest challenge for the current development of artificial intelligence lies in the amount of data, how to allow developers to have more data to improve the accuracy of their own model industry pain points, use blockchain incentives to build productivity (artificial intelligence) and production factors (large Data) bridge between. OpenAIChain will become the world's largest data collection platform, enabling users across the entire network to contribute data to the entire population through blockchain incentive policies and balance user data nodes.

    Build AI knowledge sharing, question and answer platform

    OpenAIChain, by closely following the artificial intelligence talent training pain points, does not have enough open source algorithm sharing and algorithm exchange features, builds open source community software running on the blockchain, rewards knowledge sharers through blockchain incentive policies, and forms its own AI development community. AI algorithm warehouse.

    Tracing the source of college papers

    OpenAIChain aims to construct traceability of the thesis, run machine learning thesis project through the central node, and use the OpenAIChain smart contract system to judge the training accuracy, storage model and training parameters, improve the credibility of journal papers, and eliminate the phenomenon of fraud in college papers.

    Shared computing power and storage platform

    OpenAIChain provides a consensus-based, distributed node computing and storage AI infrastructure platform through node campaigning solutions, which frees AI developers, college users, and scientific researchers from device imaging training.

    Model and Algorithm Trading Platform

    OpenAIChain creates algorithms, model transactions, and program trading platforms. It uses knowledge-paying ideas to make the algorithm no longer idle on the computer's hard disk, but instead plays its role in specific areas. AI developers can earn considerable revenue through the platform. The trading platform is certified through the official smart contract system to ensure transaction reliability.

    Cross-domain multi-party cooperation platform

    OpenAIChain is committed to complementing multidisciplinary talents, building links between universities and research institutes, integrating the field of artificial intelligence with multiple disciplines, and integrating the natural science papers of universities and artificial intelligence to enhance scientific research exchanges in Chinese universities.

    Artificial Intelligence Education Project

    OpenAIChain aims to trace the origin of individual education, collaborate with primary and secondary schools in various regions, and record the growth trajectory of students. Records, scientific analysis of students' personal achievement pain points. Work with regional education bureaus to create blockchain landing projects.
