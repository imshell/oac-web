    Creating Shared Artificial Intelligence Platform based on Blockchain

    OpenAIChain

    Overview

    OpenAiChain is a next-generation blockchain platform that aims to link blockchain technology and artificial intelligence,which established a
    platform for collecting data with incentives for all human beings as well as traceability platform for academic papers and an artificial
    intelligence algorithm knowledge sharing platform
    • National Data Acquisition Platform
    • Knowledge sharing as well as Q&A platform
    • Tracing the source of college papers
    • Shared computing power and storage platform
    • Model and Algorithm Trading Platform
    • Multi-party cooperation platform
    • Artificial Intelligence Education Project

    National Data Acquisition Platform

    According to the data of one of the three rickshaws in the current artificial intelligence, how to make enterprises and developers have more pain points in the industry, such as model training data, and use blockchain incentive policies to build up productivity (artificial intelligence) and production factors (big data). bridge. OpenAIChain will become the world's largest data collection platform to solve the problem of monopoly of industry data. At the same time, companies can publish data collection tasks on the platform to obtain specific data.

    Knowledge Sharing, Q&A Platform

    For current AI beginners who lack sufficient algorithms to share and exchange algorithms, OpenAIChain builds knowledge sharing software that runs on the blockchain, rewards knowledge-sharers through blockchain incentive policies, and forms an AI development community on the chain, and an AI algorithm warehouse.

    Traceability Platform for Academic Papers

    For deep learning, machine learning papers difficult to reproduce, academic journal fraud events, OpenAIChain aims to construct the traceability platform of the paper, determine the training accuracy through intelligent contract system, store the model and its training parameters on the chain, improve the credibility of journal papers, and eliminate The phenomenon of fraud in college papers.

    Shared computing power and storage Platform

    Construct a distributed node computing cluster and storage data base platform through the node election program, so that AI developers, college users, and scientific research workers can train their own models without the influence of training equipment.

    Model and Algorithm Trading Platform

    Build an algorithm, model, and program trading platform. With the idea of knowledge payment, let the program no longer be idle on the computer hard disk, and play its role in specific fields. AI developers can obtain considerable income through the platform. The trading platform is certified through the official smart contract system to ensure transaction reliability.

    Multi-party Cooperation Platform

    Committed to the complementarity of multi-disciplinary talents, to create a platform for contact between universities, research institutes. The combination of artificial intelligence and natural science projects will increase the exchange of scientific research in Chinese universities and train more AI talents for enterprises.

    Artificial Intelligence Education Project

    Collaborate with the regional education bureaus to trace the origin of individual education, collaborate with primary and secondary schools in all regions, record the growth trajectory, analyze and dig out the pain points of students, improve student achievement, and cultivate artificial intelligence talents since childhood.
